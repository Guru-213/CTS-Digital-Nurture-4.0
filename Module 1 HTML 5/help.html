Help Center

Navigation

Use the top navigation bar to access different sections like Home, Events, and Contact.

Registration

Fill out the event registration form with your details. All fields marked as required must be completed.

Feedback

Enter your feedback in the provided text area. Character count updates automatically as you type.

Preferences

Your selected event type is saved to your browser. You can clear this using the "Clear Preferences" button.

Location

Click "Find Nearby Events" to use your device's GPS and see your coordinates. Ensure location access is allowed.
